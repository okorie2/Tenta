What We Do

Road Construction

We speciaplize in the building of roads, highways, interchanges, and associated utilities. Our services include sealcoating, asphalt paving, concrete paving, new road construction, road rehabilitation, marking, and striping.

Polyvinyl Chloride Production

We have our specialization in a variety of products and have been recognized as one of the leading manufacturer, supplier, and exporter of PVC Film in Nigeria because of the flawless quality of our product.

Chemical Import/Export

We provide the building blocks for a wide range of products, from packaging materials and plastic bottles to automobile bumpers, synthetic rubber, solvents and countless consumer goods manufactured in Nigeria and othe West African countries.

Engineering Projects

We provide Engineering, Procurement, Construction, Installation & Commissioning services to our clients.We aim to support our clients from the early phase of projects right through the entire development of the field

Erosion Monitoring

As experts of erosion control and monitoring, and shore protection, we pride ourselves with offering top-notch services to our clients and partners. We not only import erosion control materials but also but also offer exceptional consultancy services in the area.

Glass Reinforced Plastic Fabrication

As one of the reputable manufacturers of high quality fiberglass reinforced plastic (FRP/GRP) products in Nigeria, our capabilities are extensive as we have constantly pushed beyond our limits through years of first-hand experience and research.
