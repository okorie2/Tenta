TENTA rewards engineering entrepreneurs at Catalyst event

Sep 26, 2017

Fisayo Grace Akinola, a TENTA projectYEP entrepreneur emerged as winner of the idea projection contest organized for young entrepreneurs in engineering business as part of the Catalyst event held in Port Harcourt, taking home five hundred thousand Naira prize money to boost her business.

EXAN awards TENTA for local content development

Sep 05, 2017

The Export Association of Nigeria (EXAN) presented the Odion Otis Award for Outstanding Performance to TENTA company Nigeria limited in commendation of their excellent inputs to the improvement of local content in the Nigerian chemical import/export sector.

TENTA, Gibs partner for lift systems supply

Sep 05, 2017

TENTA has taken up partnership with Ghanian engineering company, Gibs Limited, for the provision of quality lift systems better equipped for user safety and ensure usage efficiency. The partnership is also geared to improve maintenance and manning of lifts and lift systems.

TENTA donates mosquito nets to families of communities in Bayelsa state

Nov 15, 2017

TENTA Company Nigeria Limited continues to facilitate advancement and impact lives in the nation’s communities, living up to its true principles as a firm promoter and believer in the welfare of citizens and residents in the country. In the company’s commitment to its corporate social responsibility, TENTA provided long-lasting treated mosquito nets targeted at protecting and maintaining the health and physical integrity of about 1000 families in communities in Bayelsa State.
