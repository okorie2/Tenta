Tenta launches the Rural Community Development Project (RCDP)

Sep 13, 2021

The Rural Community Development Project is targeted at addressing the issue of food security, whoile promoting sustainable farm-based livelihoods for people in rural communities in Rivers State.

The key activities of the projects are:

Rural Community Development: To ensure holistic development of villages and its people, the project concentrates on enhancing livelihood, health, early childhood development, youth related activities, education, water & sanitation, environment, and strengthening grassroots institutions.

Integrated Farm-based Development:The project also introduces a platforms such as Help Groups and Farmers Clubs where institutions can work together with rural farmers. The group members receive focused training on sustainable agricultural practices and animal husbandry to improve their productivity, income, and quality of life.

Skills & Vocational Training: The project also supports non-farm livelihoods, skills and vocational training, where youth are trained on income generating and vocational skills like computer applications, tailoring, beautician, handicrafts, machine repair, among others
