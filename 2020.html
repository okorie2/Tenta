COVID-19: Hope for testing as TENTA delivers Donations 2020

Sep 26, 2020

Testing for COVID-19 has been made possible in some hospitals and health facilities in Rivers State as TENTA Company Nigeria Limited donates PCR machines among many other medical equipment. Gabriel Okon, Chief Consultant at one of the beneficiary facilities describes the donations as a timely intervention to aid in the management of spread of the virus.

Tenta Company Nigeria Limited Donates Palliatives to Communities and Hospital in Rivers and Bayelsa States

Oct 06, 2020

Tenta Company Nigeria Limited had made a series of corporate social responsibility contributions including food supplies, personal protective equipment and mattress donations amongst others to local councils, communities, hospitals; and government-nominated Covid-19 isolation centres across the country since the corona virus pandemic commenced. Tenta, in fulfilment of the company’s Managing Director, Dr. Femi Adeyemi pledge to continue to assist the government and people of Nigeria to the best of its ability in the fight to contain the spread and impact of Covid-19, again last Monday, donated large quantities of face masks and gloves to more health care facilities.

TENTA Donates more Palliatives along Communities and Hospital in Rivers State

Sep 35, 2020

TENTA Company Nigeria Limited has made a number of corporate social responsibility donations including food supplies, personal protective equipment, clinical beds and mattress contributions to local councils, communities and hospitals in Rivers state. This action was in response to healthcare and nutritional needs that been on the steady increase since the the outbreak of the pandemic in the state and country at large.

Nov 05, 2020

On Thursday, May 12, 2020, TENTA Company Nigeria Limited donated beds for hospitals with mattresses, to the University of Port Harcourt Teaching Hospital (UPTH) in Rivers State to support in the shoring-up of hospital resources mequired to tackle the COVID-19 pandemic.
